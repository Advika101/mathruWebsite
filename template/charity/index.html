Our Work

Mathru Foundation has started its functions in 2005 in the quarter (rented house) provided by the HAL Management. Currently Mathru Foundation is serving only for the physically disabled children by providing food, shelter and medical support along with their overall development. All these services are given to them free of cost. The contributions/donations is the only source of income from the public who have recognized and appreciated our work for the last 8 years. We have not yet received any grants or help from the Government for our activities.
